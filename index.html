About Legacy House Sports

We’re a boutique athlete representation & advisory firm built by marketers, dealmakers, former athletes, and coaches.

That’s intentional.

We exist to build a bridge between top performers & the brand and communications opportunities that have traditionally been transactional, opaque, and intimidating — especially for young athletes and their families navigating this for the first time.

Our job isn’t just to connect athletes with opportunities.

It’s to help both sides of that equation find the right opportunities, understand them, prepare for them, and grow from them.

How we do that isn't rocket science.

We get to know our clients at a personal level, find authentic alignments and then make sure the deal is clear, fair, and equitable.

Building relationships.
Authentic activations and alignment.
Trust that's earned.

The Legacy House Sports Point of View

The old model treats athletes like inventory.

We don’t.

We believe representation should be:

  • Personal, not volume-driven
  • Protective, and without pressure
  • Educational, not confusing
  • Strategic, and focused on long-term

We’re here to modernize the experience — without losing sight of the people at the center of it.

What Makes Legacy House Sports Different

Boutique by Design

We’ve chosen to work with a small, selective group of athletes so we can be deeply involved in every relationship.

That means more access, more context, and more care.

Brand-Smart Representation

Our background is in marketing, sales, and communications. We know how brands evaluate partnerships, what makes an athlete marketable, how to build and leverage that influence, and how to position competitors for long-term value — not just one deal.

Coaching Comes Standard

Especially for NIL and first-time partnerships, we help athletes learn the ropes — how deals work, what questions to ask, and how to show up professionally and authentically.

Family-Friendly, Athlete-First

For younger athletes, we work closely with families to create clarity and confidence during an often overwhelming process.

No smoke. No pressure. No surprises.
